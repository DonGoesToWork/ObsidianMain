**Separate Post – Staffing Change – Implementation of Activity Requirements**

Disclaimer: This is a suggestion only and I do not intend to implement this without consent from other admins. I also want to emphasize that this section is NOT intended to exclude any specific person from any staff level. It is only to ensure that people within their given roles are fulfilling the duties of their roles. Also, even if we were to implement this suggestion, I wouldn’t implement it right away. I definitely want to prove that I am going to be actively developing the server again first, so this is really a ‘get feedback early’ type of situation.

There have been a few points that have been made in the server. And, I want to bring them all up beforehand for context.

On my part, I said that I don’t intend to demote anybody from staff roles without reason ([https://discord.com/channels/283020411346878465/780499743708414052/935204086494687372](https://discord.com/channels/283020411346878465/780499743708414052/935204086494687372)).

In that same conversation timeframe roughly, Courtney expressed a belief that many admins and no staff would look strange.

IsoCat also said a bit above that that he’d never request to be demoted either. And, at a different time, he has expressed that he can’t really devote time to the server for development.

I’ve thought long and hard about these facts and decided that I want to make a change to the Staff Requirements of the community. I want there to be activity requirements for all levels of staff roles, where you are expected to activity contribute in some meaningful way affiliated with your role ‘occasionally’.

I do not want to specifically define what ‘occasionally’ is, because again, I don’t want this to be some kind of super strict requirement where if you don’t log in daily you get demoted. At the same time, if you’ve accepted a role to assist the server as a volunteer, I don’t think it’s unreasonable to ask for you to contribute to that role occasionally. And, if you can’t or are unwilling, that you temporarily or permanently (if you so decide) to step down from the role until you can.

Again, I don’t want this to come off as specifically targeting any single person. So, to be clear, even if we do decide to move in this direction, any person will be given more than enough time to meet the ‘occasional’ requirement, whatever it is. And, even if they don’t meet the requirement, or decide they aren’t interested in it, we can work out arrangements to allow them to feel included. For instance, I am entirely unopposed to a non-obvious unofficial admin-like group if appearances are really our only concern.

I repeat. I am completely OK with people ‘not having the admin role’ but having ‘admin rights’. I’ve even thought of a cool discord group name for it already called ‘OG’ in orange. 😎

This would only be only for our current group of admins though, something to never be seen again in the future. And, I don’t intend to extend it to non-admin staff members.

So… yeah. Kind of a hard topic for me to write about because, in a perfect world, I wouldn’t demote anybody. But, I do think that this kind of change would be beneficial for the greater good, so it’s something that should be discussed.

What do you all think? And, if you think this is a possibly good idea, what might ‘occasionally’ look like to you? Once a week? Once a month? ONCE A YEAR!?

Of course, feel free to tell me if you think its crap and I can delete my post and we can pretend it never happened.
