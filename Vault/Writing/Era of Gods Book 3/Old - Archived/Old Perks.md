

Shield Wall: Gain the ability to use Shield Wall while holding up a shield. Shield Wall improves your ability to block with a shield by 10% [+0.4% per level] for as long as you hold your shield up. Has 3 [+1 per 10 levels] uses that recover over 24 hours.
Protector: You are 10% more effective at intercepting abilities aimed at allies.
Inspiring Shout I – Gain the ability to produce an Inspiring Shout. Allies that hear the shout will gain bonuses based no their distance from the shout. The maximum effect is +2 [+2 per level] strength, dexterity and vitality and +2% [+2% confidence per level] per use. Being higher leveled improves the effects of the shout, it’s duration and range. 2 [+1 per 5 levels] Inspiring Shouts may be used per 24 hours.

Lawful Good Curse: You have a lawful good alignment and must act generally in accordance with this morality. Your personality will be forcibly skewed toward this alignment. Gain +2 perk points.

Warrior Specialization Curse: You are particularly disadvantaged with every non-warrior themed action. Gain +2 perk points.

Bait Curse: All tracking, analysis and investigation attempts against you will gain substantial bonuses to success, effectiveness and accuracy.


War Leader I - By activating this perk, you enter a 10 minute period of meditation to gather power that can be interrupted early if desired. Once the countdown reaches zero, acquire the state of War Leader. War Leader grants numerous combat-focused benefits while activated and lasts for 2 hours. All entities in an excessively large radius can intrinsically sense your meditation and understand that you will achieve the War Leader state upon completion based on intelligence. Has 1 [+1 per 100 levels] charge that recovers over 24 hours.

Onslaught I - Regenerate 1% of your physical fatigue per second, regenerate 100% of your health per day (0.001158334% health per second) and increase your maximum martial force utilization threshold by 10% [+1% per level]
