# Overview

- World name is Anaria.
- 50 million mile wide planet.
- Contains vast underground world and dungeons.
- Contains portals that lead to mystery realms inside of dungeons.
- Invaded by sky monsters frequently.

# Major Land Masses

Anaria is divided into numerous major landmasses relevant to our story. The main area that the story is set in is the "Moran Region" which includes several major continents, like the Galgas Continent.

Area Breakdown:
- 120 major areas in the level 1-20 bracket.
- 40 areas in the 20-40 bracket.
- 20 in the 40-60 bracket.
- 3 in the 60-80 bracket.
- 1 in the 80+ bracket.

Story begins in [[Galgas Overview]], a level 1-20 territory.
